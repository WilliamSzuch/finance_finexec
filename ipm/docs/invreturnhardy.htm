invReturnHardy (monad)

Form: explicit
Calculate the rate of investmnet return using Hardy's
approximation formula over one time unit.

Syntax

  invReturnHardy(Vb;Ve;I)
Vb = value of assets at beginning of time unit including accruals
Ve = value of assets at end of time unit including accurals
I  = investment income over the time unit including
